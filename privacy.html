Privacy & Policy

Kiyomi Spa Centre, we value and respect your privacy. Our commitment to protecting your personal information is fundamental to ensuring a trusted and secure experience when you visit our spa. We collect only the necessary personal details, such as your name, contact information, and medical history, to tailor our services to your individual needs and ensure your safety during treatments. This information is securely stored and used solely for the purpose of providing exceptional spa services and enhancing your overall experience. We will never share, sell, or rent your personal details to third parties without your explicit consent, except as required by law. We implement strict measures to safeguard your data, using secure servers and encryption technologies. Additionally, any communications you receive from us, such as promotional offers or appointment reminders, will always be sent with your consent, and you may opt-out at any time. We are committed to maintaining the confidentiality of your information and providing a safe environment for you to relax and rejuvenate. By visiting Kiyomi Spa Centre, you acknowledge and agree to our privacy policy and the measures we take to protect your personal data. Should you have any questions or concerns regarding your privacy, please feel free to contact us at any time.


  • 1. Personal Information Collection: We collect only the necessary personal information, such as your name, contact details, and medical history, to provide tailored spa services and ensure your safety during treatments.
  • 2. Data Security: All personal information is securely stored and protected using industry-standard encryption and secure server technology to prevent unauthorized access.
  • 3. Use of Information: Your information is used exclusively to deliver the services you request, such as appointment scheduling, personalized treatments, and communication regarding spa offers.
  • 4. Confidentiality: We will never share, sell, or rent your personal data to third parties without your explicit consent, unless required by law.
  • 5. Communication Preferences: Any promotional offers, updates, or appointment reminders are sent only with your consent. You have the option to opt-out of receiving such communications at any time.
  • 6. Third-Party Links: Our website may contain links to third-party sites, but we are not responsible for their privacy practices. We encourage you to review their policies before sharing any personal information.
  • 7. Data Retention: We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected or as required by law.
  • 8. Access & Control: You have the right to access, update, or correct your personal information. If you wish to do so, please contact us directly.
  • 9. Cookies: Our website may use cookies to improve your browsing experience. By using our site, you consent to the use of cookies.
  • 10. Policy Updates: We may update our privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on our website, and we encourage you to review it periodically.
