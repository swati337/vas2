Shiatsu Massage

Shiatsu Massage is a traditional Japanese therapy that uses finger pressure on specific points along the body’s energy pathways. This technique helps to relieve tension, improve circulation, and promote relaxation by restoring balance to the body. It is deeply calming and can also alleviate stress, headaches, and muscle pain.

Kobido Facial

Kobido Facial is an ancient Japanese facial massage technique that combines acupressure, lymphatic drainage, and skin lifting techniques. This treatment promotes youthful, glowing skin by stimulating blood circulation and relaxing facial muscles. It helps to reduce fine lines, improve skin elasticity, and relieve facial tension.
