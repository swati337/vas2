Shiatsu Massage

Shiatsu Massage is a traditional Japanese therapy that uses finger pressure on specific points along the body’s energy pathways. This technique helps to relieve tension, improve circulation, and promote relaxation by restoring balance to the body. It is deeply calming and can also alleviate stress, headaches, and muscle pain.

Kobido Facial

Kobido Facial is an ancient Japanese facial massage technique that combines acupressure, lymphatic drainage, and skin lifting techniques. This treatment promotes youthful, glowing skin by stimulating blood circulation and relaxing facial muscles. It helps to reduce fine lines, improve skin elasticity, and relieve facial tension.

Ashiatsu Foot Massage

A Foot Spa offers a relaxing and rejuvenating experience for your feet, combining warm water soaks, exfoliation, and soothing massages. This treatment helps to relieve stress, improve circulation, and soften rough skin. Ideal for relaxation, a foot spa promotes overall well-being while leaving your feet feeling refreshed and pampered.

Onsen Bath

An onsen bath is a traditional Japanese hot spring bath, known for its therapeutic properties. The mineral-rich waters, sourced from natural hot springs, help soothe muscles, improve circulation, and promote relaxation. Onsen baths offer a serene and rejuvenating experience, often set in picturesque natural environments.

Matcha Body Scrub

A Matcha body scrub combines the antioxidant-rich benefits of matcha green tea with exfoliating ingredients to gently remove dead skin cells. This scrub helps brighten and hydrate the skin, leaving it soft and rejuvenated. It also offers soothing and anti-inflammatory properties for a refreshed, glowing complexion.

Bamboo Massage

Bamboo massage uses smooth, heated bamboo rods to apply pressure and knead the muscles, promoting deep relaxation. The technique helps relieve tension, improve circulation, and reduce stress. This unique massage provides a soothing, invigorating experience, enhancing overall well-being.
